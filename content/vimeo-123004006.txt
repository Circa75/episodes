On Melancholy

March 23rd, 2015

Melancholy doesn’t mean grim and miserable… it means grasping without rage the fact that the world is full of folly and greed, that it is rare to find inner peace, that is is hard to live comfortably with those who we love, that it’s very unusual to find a career that’s both financially rewarding and morally uplifting and that many decent people in this world have a very hard time.
